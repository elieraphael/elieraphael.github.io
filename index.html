I work at UMR Gulliver 7083, a laboratory located at the The City of Paris Industrial Physics and Chemistry Higher Educational Institution ESPCI.
My research focuses on the physics of soft matter and interfaces. In particular, I am interested in the behavior of polymers at interfaces, elastocapillarity phenomena, the propagation of capillary gravity waves, and the dynamics of granular materials.

I am a Fellow of the American Physical Society (2015)
 
For my teaching activities at ESPCI, see here.
